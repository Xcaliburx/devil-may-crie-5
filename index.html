About

Devil May Cry 5 is a 2019 action-adventure game developed and published by Capcom.
It is the sixth installment overall and the fifth mainline installment in the Devil
May Cry series. Capcom released it for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and
Xbox One on 8 March 2019.
